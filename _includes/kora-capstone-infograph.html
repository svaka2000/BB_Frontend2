About

Kora is an operations backbone that executes maintenance automatically. Built for property managers and institutional owners, it converts unstructured inputs like SMS, emails, and photos into structured maintenance actions.

Impact

  • Reduces maintenance coordination costs by 70%+
  • 24/7 automation
  • Scales portfolios without scaling headcount
  • Institutional-grade security and audit trails
